Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 27th, 2024

Overview

This Privacy Policy outlines how Neoneko ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our websites (neoneko.xyz, catsblenderplugin.xyz, and avatartoolkit.xyz) and services. We prioritize your privacy and maintain full transparency in our data practices.

Information Collection

We minimize data collection and only gather essential information:

  • IP Address: Logged by our web server and CDN for security and operational purposes
  • Browser Information: Basic details about your browser and operating system to optimize website performance

Browser Storage

We use local storage exclusively for:

  • Storing your theme preferences
  • Improving your browsing experience

This data remains on your device and isn't transmitted to our servers.

Content Delivery Network

We utilize Bunny CDN to deliver content efficiently. Their services may collect standard technical data for operational purposes. For detailed information, please refer to Bunny CDN's privacy policy.

Data Usage & Sharing

We:

  • Never sell or trade your information
  • Only use collected data for website functionality and security
  • Share data only with essential service providers (like our CDN)

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request information about any data we might have
  • Clear local storage through your browser settings
  • Additional rights under GDPR if you're in the EEA

Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted here with an updated revision date.
